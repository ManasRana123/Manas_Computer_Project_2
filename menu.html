Sunset Vista Grill Menu


Appetizers:

  • Sun-Kissed Bruschetta
    Fresh tomatoes, basil, garlic, and balsamic reduction on toasted baguette slices.
  • Golden Shrimp Ceviche
    Succulent shrimp marinated in citrus juices, cilantro, red onion, and served with tortilla chips.
  • Mediterranean Mezze Platter
    Hummus, tzatziki, roasted red pepper dip, olives, feta, and warm pita bread.

Salads:


  • Fields of Greens
    Mixed greens, strawberries, candied walnuts, goat cheese, and balsamic vinaigrette.
  • Ocean Breeze Salad
    Grilled shrimp, mixed greens, avocado, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and citrus herb dressing.
  • Sunset Caprese
    Fresh mozzarella, heirloom tomatoes, basil, drizzled with olive oil and balsamic glaze.

Main Courses


  • Seared Salmon with Mango Salsa
    Pan-seared salmon fillet served with a tropical mango and pineapple salsa, accompanied by sautéed asparagus.
  • Grilled Ribeye Steak
    Juicy ribeye steak seasoned to perfection, served with garlic mashed potatoes and roasted vegetables.
  • Herb-Infused Chicken Supreme
    Grilled chicken breast with a medley of herbs, served with wild rice pilaf and lemon butter sauce.
  • Vegetable Medley Risotto
    Creamy Arborio rice cooked with seasonal vegetables, parmesan, and a touch of white wine.

From the Sea:


  • Lobster Ravioli
    Homemade ravioli filled with lobster and ricotta, tossed in a light creamy tomato sauce.
  • Pan-Seared Scallops
    Seared scallops served on a bed of saffron-infused couscous, with roasted red pepper coulis.

Sweet Endings:


  • Sunset Bliss Crème Brûlée
    Classic vanilla bean custard with a caramelized sugar crust, served with fresh berries.
  • Warm Chocolate Lava Cake
    Decadent chocolate cake with a molten center, accompanied by vanilla bean ice cream.
  • Tropical Fruit Parfait Layers of tropical fruits, coconut yogurt, and granola, drizzled with honey.

Beverages:


  • Sunset Sangria
    A refreshing blend of red wine, fresh fruits, and a hint of brandy.
  • Mango Mint Splash
    Fresh mango and mint muddled together, topped with sparkling water.
  • Cucumber Basil Martini
    A refreshing cocktail with cucumber, basil, gin, and a touch of lime.
  • Sunset Vista Iced Tea
    A mix of black tea, passion fruit, and citrus, served over ice.

Wine Selection:


Our carefully curated wine list features a variety of reds, whites, and rosés from renowned vineyards around the world. Our knowledgeable staff will assist you in choosing the perfect pairing for your meal.
